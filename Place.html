Place

The hockey rinks I play at are cold and have metal bleachers inside the rink. Outside they have a lobby type area with a bar and grill. Upstairs there is more seating this area is heated and has windows to see what's going on on the ice. The rinks all almost always have 2 ice rinks or sheets of ice.
