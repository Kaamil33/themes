Human Environment Interaction (HEI)

The environment is cold on ice because the cold helps us not boil in all of our equipment. Rubber is used to make our game pucks and the pucks we use on the ice and plastic for our street hockey pucks and balls. Wood is used to make some hockey sticks others are made out of fiberglass there are other materials but not as common to me. The fiberglass is a part of most hockey sticks since the 100% fiberglass sticks had a bad feel and terrible durability. Our skates are made of metal blades,leather, nylon, plastic, steel, and other synthetic materials.
