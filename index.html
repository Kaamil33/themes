How does self-awareness help learners find their unique passions and connect their passions to learning on their journey to finding their element

Self awareness helps you find yourself and how you are unique along with how school is helping with your journey to find your element. Having self awareness allows you to understand and find new passions along with improve and better understand your other passions. School can help a lot with this it might present you opportunities for sports that are played around you or that you enjoy or it might present you with volunteering opportunities to help your community and better yourself. For example I participated in a volunteering opportunity through my middle school and I loved it and found a new passion.In fact eery time the organizer needs volunteers they text me. So you never know what might inspire your passion which is why you always have to be aware of what you are doing and what you can do find a new passion or better understand your current passions. Geography is everywhere and you can find it in anything you do including your passions by finding the 5 themes of geography within it.
